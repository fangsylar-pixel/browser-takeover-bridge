# Community Code of Conduct

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Harassment, threats, discrimination, deliberate
disclosure of private browser data, and abusive behavior are not accepted.

Project maintainers may edit or remove content that creates safety, privacy, or legal risk and may
limit participation when needed to protect the community.

Report sensitive conduct or security concerns privately when possible. Public reports should never
contain credentials, private documents, signed URLs, or other authenticated browser content.
